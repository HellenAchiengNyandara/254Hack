About the Project

The Food Price Predictor is an advanced machine learning system designed to predict agricultural commodity prices in Kenya. By analyzing historical price data, seasonal patterns, market conditions, and other relevant variables, our model can predict future price trends with high accuracy.

Project Goals

Accurate Forecasting

Provide accurate price prediction for agricultural commodities to help stakeholders make informed decisions.

Farmer Support

Help farmers make informed decisions about planting, harvesting, and selling their produce.

Food Security

Reduce price volatility and improve food security in Kenya through better market planning.
